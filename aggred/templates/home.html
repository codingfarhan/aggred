Who We Are


Aggred aims to create a strong community around Education that allows students, teachers and everyone else who is a part of this beautiful process to find help and opportunity to help others in the community.

We plan to do this by providing a platform where people in the community can interact so it transforms into the go-to place for people to find help and guidance with their problems. We believe that co-dependence is the key to building a successful and blossoming community.

What We Offer

Forum for Discussions

You can discuss any topic related to Education. There is a good chance you will be able to find someone with a similar doubt or relevant knowledge to help you.

Place to build a Reputation

You can create your profile to participate in discussions. Useful answers earn you crowns, which builds your credibility and reputation on the platform!

Opportunity to Network

Discussions lead to new friendships, which can in turn lead to new career opportunities. Beware of Scammers and people who don't have good intentions.
